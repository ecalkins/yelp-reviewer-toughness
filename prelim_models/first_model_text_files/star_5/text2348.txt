0	The amazing pizza at Secret Pizza gives me reason alone to come to Vegas and I look forward to stopping by after a trip to the dayclub or a night out during every visit. The pizza is incredibly close, if not better, than the pizza in New York, with a thin but chewy crust, melty cheese, and quality toppings. Line gets pretty gnarly so expect a wait, but luckily, since it's just fast food, it tends to move pretty fast.
