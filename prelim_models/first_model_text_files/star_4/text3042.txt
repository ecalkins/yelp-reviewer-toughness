0	IF you find this place (and you'll probably have to ask someone in the hotel), you'll be pleasantly surprised by the understated, throw-back vibe of what feels like your local pizzeria.  You have to get the white pizza, no matter what.  It is amazingly tasty and full of garlic, cheesy goodness.  There are no tables, so be prepared to stand and eat or to take-and-go.
