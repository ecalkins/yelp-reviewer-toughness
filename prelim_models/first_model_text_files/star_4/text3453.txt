0	This was such a great find at the Cosmo in Vegas! It really is the most perfect late night snack after drinking. Beware, the lines are long at night! That was really the only downside. The pizza is really good...I like that they stick it back in the oven for a minute when you order a slice. I also liked that I could add on jalapenos to a simple cheese pizza. You can also order an entire pizza if you want more than individual slices.
